During my Ph.D, I worked with Prof. Toshiyuki Amagasa on making use of machine learning and computational linguistics to reason about language and knowledge. I focused on question answering over knowledge base systems.

Iam currently interested in large language models (LLMs), transformers, understanding their memory, what they remember, how they remember and generalization.
